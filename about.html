Sofia

Sofia was born in Abbiategrasso, a small town mere minutes from bustling Milan, Italy. At sixteen she began an apprenticeship in the city, and at eighteen she took on a series of jobs in Venice, Florence, and Rome, each more prestigious than the last. During her second tenure in Florence she took a weekend vacation to Lyon, where she met Gabriel. The two were immediately inseparable.

Gabriel

Gabriel was born, raised, and trained in Lyon, France, learning his craft under the tutelage of legendary Chef Paul Bocuse. One week after meeting Sofia, he left Lyon to join her in Florence, and the two soon eloped and immigrated to America. After an extended tour of both American coasts, the Durands made Providence, Rhode Island their new home. Shortly thereafter Pronto Letzgeaux welcomed its first guests.
