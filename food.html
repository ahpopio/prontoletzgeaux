Our Menu

The Chefs Durand devise the menu for Pronto Letzgeaux each morning, taking special care to accommodate the dietary concerns of the evening's guests. Ingredients are locally sourced whenever possible, and many vegetal elements are grown and harvested by the chefs themselves.

Meals typically include cocktails, hors d'oeuvres, first and second courses, small plates or salads, dessert, and a digestif, with paired wines throughout.
