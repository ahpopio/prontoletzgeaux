Pronto Letzgeaux offers its visitors the rare opportunity to join two acclaimed young chefs at the dinner table for an evening of Franco-Italian cuisine. Chefs Sofia and Gabriel Durand accompany their dinner guests throughout the evening, providing insight, instruction, and entertainment throughout. Guests at Pronto Letzgeaux dine like friends and family around a single ten-seat table, and dinner typically comprises seven courses. Anticipate joining the Durands for two to three hours and making a few new friends in the process.

Chef Gabriel was born, raised, and trained in Lyon, France, where he met Chef Sofia, a chef's apprentice on vacation from Milan, Italy. Their cuisine is a celebration of their backgrounds, marrying the nuanced French style with the passionate Italian. Diners can expect alternation between French and Italian dishes, with the primo and secondo implementing both methods for truly singular results.

Reservations are handled by the Durands themselves and can be requested through the form on the Seats page. Sofia and Gabriel will work to accommodate your requests and will contact you as quickly as possible.
